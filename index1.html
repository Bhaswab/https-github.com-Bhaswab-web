About Me

My name is Bhaswab Jyoti Goswami. I hold an M Phil in Psychiatric Social Work from LGB Regional Institute of Mental Health. Currently, I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Social Work at Assam Don Bosco University. My areas of expertise include psychotherapeutic interventions on substance use and family therapy.

Research Interests

I am passionate about exploring various psychotherapeutic interventions for substance use and family therapy. My research focuses on developing effective methods to support individuals and families in overcoming mental health challenges and improving their overall well-being.
